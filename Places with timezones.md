
## Target Cities by Timezone & IST Calling Window

By dialing from 8:30 PM to 10:30 PM IST, you can perfectly ride the 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM morning wave across the Eastern and Central timezones without staying up past midnight.

### Eastern Standard Time (EST)

**Your Calling Window:** 8:30 PM – 9:30 PM IST (Hits their 10:00 AM – 11:00 AM)

* **US Cities:** New York City (NY), Buffalo (NY), Boston (MA), Worcester (MA), Philadelphia (PA), Pittsburgh (PA), Atlanta (GA), Savannah (GA), Miami (FL), Orlando (FL), Tampa (FL), Jacksonville (FL), Charlotte (NC), Raleigh (NC), Washington (DC), Baltimore (MD), Richmond (VA), Detroit (MI), Grand Rapids (MI), Columbus (OH), Cleveland (OH), Cincinnati (OH), Indianapolis (IN), Louisville (KY), Charleston (SC), Providence (RI).
